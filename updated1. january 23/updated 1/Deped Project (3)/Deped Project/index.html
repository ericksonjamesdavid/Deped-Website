Curriculum

DEPED LAUNCHES REVISED K-10 CURRICULUM EFFECTIVE SY 2024-2025 : The Department of Education (Deped) launched the MATATAG curriculum on August 10, 2023. The curriculum is a revised version of the k to 12 curriculum that focuses on essential elements like language, reading, and math. The curriculum is designed to prepare students for the challenges they will face in the future.
